# OSINT Investigation Summary

This report provides a summary of the Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) gathered on the top attacking IP addresses identified in the Honeypot Attack Summary Report.

### IP Address Summaries:

*   **196.25.125.58:**
    *   **Location:** South Africa
    *   **Network:** Managed by AFRINIC (Regional Internet Registry for Africa).
    *   **Findings:** No immediate flags on public blacklists or direct links to specific malicious campaigns were found during the initial investigation. Further targeted queries would be needed for a definitive assessment.

*   **120.55.160.161:**
    *   **Location:** Hangzhou, China
    *   **Network:** Alibaba Cloud Computing.
    *   **Findings:** Major threat intelligence platforms show no history of malicious activity. The IP address is likely used for benign cloud hosting purposes.

*   **5.39.12.192:**
    *   **Location:** France
    *   **Network:** OVH SAS.
    *   **Findings:** Evidence strongly suggests this IP operates as a **honeypot**. It has a high volume of recorded events related to the Session Initiation Protocol (SIP), indicating it is intentionally attracting and monitoring VoIP-related traffic.

*   **182.10.161.232:**
    *   **Location:** Indonesia
    *   **Network:** PT Telekomunikasi Selular (Telkomsel).
    *   **Findings:** No public evidence of malicious activity was discovered. It is likely a standard IP address assigned to a customer of the telecommunications provider.

*   **170.64.232.235:**
    *   **Location:** Sydney, Australia
    *   **Network:** "CHOOPA," a cloud hosting provider.
    *   **Findings:** The investigation found no signs of malicious activity. The IP appears to be a benign component of a cloud hosting environment.
